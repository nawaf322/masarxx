Platform Features

Deprixa Plus covers every aspect of a modern courier and logistics business. From package intake to final delivery confirmation, every step is tracked, audited, and reported.

Shipment Management

Full lifecycle tracking from creation to delivery. Wizard-driven intake, automatic tracking numbers, barcode labels, and real-time status updates.

Multi-Branch Support

Manage multiple warehouse locations and distribution centers. Each branch has its own staff, shipments, couriers, and operational scope.

Role-Based Access

Granular permissions via Spatie Laravel Permissions. Five built-in roles — Super Admin, Admin, Employee, Driver, and Customer — with 97 customizable permissions.

Branded Customer Portal

Full white-label capability. Customize colors, fonts, logos, and layout. Clients access a tracking portal matching your brand identity.

Real-Time Tracking

Public tracking page for customers. Live map integration via Leaflet and Mapbox GL for delivery visualization. Status timeline with timestamps and driver notes.

Invoicing & Billing

Automatic invoice generation from shipments. Draft to Paid workflow, PDF export, tax configuration, recurring invoices, and credit notes.

Reporting & Analytics

Shipment summary, revenue, exception, and courier performance reports. Export to PDF, CSV, or Excel. Schedule automated email delivery.

Notification Engine

Email and in-app notifications for shipment events, status changes, delivery confirmations, exceptions, and invoice milestones.

Audit Logs

Every system action recorded via Spatie Activity Log. Full accountability for compliance, dispute resolution, and troubleshooting.

Software Updates

Built-in update management. Activate your license, check for updates, review changelogs, and apply with one click from Settings.

Dispatch & Route Optimization

Assign shipments to couriers individually or with the auto-optimize engine. Monitor driver locations in real time and review location history for dispute resolution.

Rate Calculator

Zone-based pricing engine with weight tiers and dimensional factors. Includes a public rate calculator at /rate — no login required — for customer self-service quotes.

Multi-Channel Invoice Delivery

Send invoices to clients via Email, WhatsApp, or SMS with a single click. PDF invoices include company branding, line items, taxes, and a payment QR code.

REST API Access

Full REST API with Bearer token authentication. Integrate with e-commerce platforms, ERPs, or customer-facing apps via documented endpoints.

Customer Self-Registration Portal

Each organization gets a unique public registration URL. Customers sign up themselves, receive an invitation, and land directly in their personal My Locker dashboard — no admin intervention needed.

Invitation System

Staff and customers are invited by email with a secure token link — no passwords set by admins. Admins can resend invitations, and the system gracefully falls back to a copyable link when SMTP is not yet configured.

Origin Pickups

Schedule and manage package pickup requests from customers or locations. Full status workflow from pending through confirmed to completed, with driver assignment and address management.

Lockers

Assign virtual or physical locker boxes to customers. Each customer gets a unique locker code and address. Integrates with Pre-Alerts for seamless international parcel management.

Pre-Alerts

Customers notify the warehouse about incoming packages before arrival. Pre-alerts are reviewed by staff and converted to shipments upon receipt — essential for international locker operations.

Returns Management

Manage return and devolution shipments linked to original orders. Dedicated permissions for viewing, creating, and updating return shipments throughout their lifecycle.

Cash on Delivery (COD)

End-to-end COD tracking. Drivers collect payment at delivery; admins reconcile and remit to senders. Fully integrated with shipment statuses and financial reporting.

Who Is Deprixa Plus For?

Deprixa Plus is designed for logistics and courier businesses that have outgrown spreadsheets and need a centralized, professional platform without building one from scratch.

Courier Companies

Companies processing 50 to 50,000 parcels per month. Manage couriers, delivery zones, vehicle assignments, and customer notifications from one dashboard.

Freight Forwarders

Freight companies managing complex shipments across multiple carriers. Track freight at the consignment level with custom service types and documentation.

E-Commerce Fulfillment

Online retailers running in-house delivery operations. Link orders to shipments, manage returns, and give customers real-time tracking links.

Last-Mile Delivery

Hyper-local delivery operations handling same-day and next-day shipments. Zone management, courier workflow, and exception handling built in.

Logistics Operators

Multi-branch logistics providers handling inbound, outbound, and returns. Full visibility across all locations with consolidated reporting and billing.

SaaS Resellers

Agencies and developers who want to white-label and resell a fully built logistics platform to multiple clients with separate branding per installation.
